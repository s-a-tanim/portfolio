I am a motivated and passionate researcher seeking to pursue a master's degree in Computer Science. I am a Research Assistant (RA) at Advanced Machine Intelligence Research Lab (AMIR Lab). I received a BSc in Computer Science and Engineering from American International University-Bangladesh (AIUB). My research focuses on machine learning and computer vision, particularly in secure federated learning and model transparency interoperability. I aim to apply these innovations to significant global challenges, including improving data privacy in climate-related research and fostering interoperability among diverse datasets. By examining the intersection of technology and sustainability, I seek to contribute to effective solutions for critical issues such as biodiversity conservation and climate resilience.

Additionally, I founded Learnify Research Lab, where we collaborate with students in Bangladesh who are interested in conducting research and work to spread research interests among them.
